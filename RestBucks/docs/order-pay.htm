Pay an order

  • Uri: order/{id}/payment
  • Method: POST
  • Preconditions: The status of the order must be unpaid. You can't pay an order twice.
  • Note: After you paying an order the barista will prepare your coffee in 45 seconds.
  • Example payload 
    <payment xmlns="http://restbuckson.net">
  <card-number>0145-12245-157487</card-number>
  <card-owner>Carlos Garcia</card-owner>
</payment>
